## WHAT HAPPENED
Jun 13, 2025 We partnered with Darren Aronofsky, Eliza McNitt and a team of more than 200 people to make a film using Veo and live-action filmmaking. Kory Mathewson Senior Research Scientist, Google DeepMind General summary Google DeepMind partnered with Primordial Soup to produce "ANCESTRA" a short film premiering at the Tribeca Festival.
