# Department of Commerce Announces Transition of Baldrige Performance Excellence Program

<!-- HERO_IMAGE: add an editorial image URL or local path here -->

## WHAT HAPPENED
Beginning with the 2026 award cycle, the Alliance for Performance Excellence and the Baldrige Foundation will take on most operations for the Baldrige Program. NIST and the Department of Commerce will maintain ultimate responsibility for the Baldrige Excellence Framework and the Baldrige Award process. The Baldrige Program has helped thousands of organizations improve, bringing better manufacturing, education, health care, products and services to millions of Americans.
